Parties are fun, but at the cost of Nature

A lot of leftover residue and general debris And left after the fun is over
Most of them are plastics and other non-biodegradable products
These non-biodegradable products release toxins and are harmful for our nature and to us

Single use disposable cutlery/crockery is one of the biggest threats to our local environment. Many residents buy “bio-degradable” cutlery but many of these are of questionable authenticity and their environmental credentials are questionable. The cleanest solution to reduce this waste is to go with re-usable cutlery for parties and get-togethers.
